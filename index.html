HOW I WORK

01
Brief & Raw Footage

I map out the narrative first — pacing, hook placement, retention points — before touching a single cut. Structure is everything.

03

Color, audio balancing, captions, motion graphics. First cut delivered, revisions incorporated. No ghost edits, no guesswork.

04

Export in your required format — platform-optimized for YouTube, Instagram, or any other. Ready to post, no extra processing needed.
